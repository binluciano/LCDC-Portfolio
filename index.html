About Us

An all-purpose company, LCDC offers a wide array of services from conception to project realization. LCDC's design portfolio does not only limit itself to structural design but it includes architectural, plumbing, mechanical and electrical designs as well.

With LCDC, our designs may also be implemented through its in-house construction services. LCDC's construction arm caters to the entire array of new building projects, renovation and even retrofitting works.
